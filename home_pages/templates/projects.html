    quickData

  • US-traded public companies' financial methodologies can vary based on industry, business model, geographic location, and company-specific preferences. Any particular company's financials can change significantly quarter to quarter, both in terms of account names, and statement structure.

    quickData is a python library which scraps the Secruities Exchange Comission (SEC) website for public filings and generates time-series data. quickData is able to create n:m mappings between different financial reports of any given publicly traded company and combine these filings to output time-series data.
  • As an ongoing project, I intend to add a record of how quickData maps different filings to one another so as to have lossless and dynamically reversible mappings.
